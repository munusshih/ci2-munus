Assignments

The assignment is broken into two parts. Please finish the following things after choosing an existing piece of text you'd like to work with.

  • Project 2-1: Concept & Data Collection

    Create a collection of 25 to 50 items.  Think about the theme of your data? What kind of data? Collect these data and label them with a unique identifier. Think about all the lables you can give them, such as colors, sizes, weights, years made, smell, emotion and etc. Prepare to discuss them in a 1-on-1 with Munus.

  • Project 2-2: Low-fidelity Prototype

    Consider how the collection is organized and experienced — one at a time, in a grid, on separate pages? Consider if there are groupings within your collection and how the relationship between typography and mixed media can add a visual perspective to your work. Use pen and paper or digital prototyping software (such as Figma) to demosntrate your concept. Prepare to discuss them in a 1-on-1 with Munus.
