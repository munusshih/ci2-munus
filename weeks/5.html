Description

This week, we will shift our focus to a pivotal aspect of web design: responsive design! We will delve into its rich history, technical evolution, diverse philosophical approaches, and its growing significance in contemporary contexts. Then, we will explore the intricacies of designing layouts with a keen understanding of flexbox, media queries, and fluid typography, in order to adapt to various devices and screen sizes.

Assignments

The assignment is broken into two parts.

  • A Responsive Polish

    Continue to refine your work by filling in all of the contents in HTML and styling them in CSS.

    Then, make your project responsive between 768px to 2560px by incorporating fluid units and media queries to ensure a seamless experience across different devices. Use the developer tools to check how your design adapts at different breakpoints.

  • Project 1 Presentation

    Develop a presentation for Project 1 that walks us through your process and idea. You will have 3 minutes to present, so think about how you can give us a succinct yet thorough walkthrough of your work. Make sure you cover the following points in your presentation:

    1. Content and Approach: Introduce the chosen content for your project and discuss your approach to re-designing the web experience of it.
    2. Site Map: Present your site map so we understand the organization and structure of your website. Walk us through the hierarchy of pages and how they connect to one another.
    3. Website: Show us your live site. Highlight key features such as menus, links, and buttons that facilitate seamless navigation for users. Discuss any design decisions you made in styling.
    4. Responsiveness: Show us your website across different dimensions using the inspect tool.
    5. Accessibility: Demonstrate how you considered accessibility in your project. Did you implement tab navigation for keyboard users? Reflect on any challenges you faced.
